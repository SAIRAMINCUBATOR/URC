Design Finalization of Rover for University Rover Challenge: Group Discussion


The University Rover Challenge is an exciting competition that requires teams to design, build, and operate a rover capable of completing various tasks. In order to successfully participate in this challenge, it’s important to have a well-designed and functioning rover.

Skills and Experience

One of the most important criteria for selecting team members is their skills and experience. We made sure that the team consists of individuals who have the necessary skills and expertise to complete the task at hand. This includes technical and soft skills such as communication, teamwork, and leadership.

To ensure the success of the team’s design, a group discussion was conducted on 14th November 2022 to finalize the design of the rover. The following are some key points that were discussed during the group discussion.

Design Requirements

The team discussed the design requirements for the rover, including its size, weight, and power requirements. The team also discussed the various tasks that the rover will need to complete and the specific requirements for each task. This helped the team to understand the constraints and requirements for the rover’s design.
