Our Previous Projects

Your purpose in life is to find your purpose and give your whole heart and soul to it

JARVIS-VISION

The JARVIS-VISION autonomous drone surveillance system is designed to address the challenges faced by the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) in the process of flood rescue operations

Autonomous Vehicle

A level 4 autonomous system that can be retro fitted to an existing electric vehicle. Currently we are deploying the system on an electric shuttle car to use within campus.

Jet Engine

We are developing a turbojet engine using a marine turbocharger for research purposes. Various attributes of turbojet engines can be effectively studied and tested using this live model.

Can Satellite

We have designed, developed and launched few Can Satellites that can record air pressure, gas composition, Radiation levels and Magnetic field data.

Autonomous underwater vehicle

We have developed an Autonomous underwater vehicle that can be used for underwater inspections of bridges, oil rigs, ports and pipelines for signs of damage.

Autonomus Pond Monitoring

We developed this system in collaboration with the Central Institute of Brackishwater (CIBA) to autonomously monitor various properties of water in aquaculture ponds.
